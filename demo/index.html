bacon.mouseover.js

Ever just really want to hear the sound of bacon frying? Well I did too, so here is this plugin for it.

Give it a whirl!

Using bacon.mouseover.js is a breeze!

  • Just add a class of .bacon to whatever element you would like to hear the sweet, sweet sound of bacon frying when you mouseover it.

  • Append this script to your page after all of your .bacon elements are declared.

    <script type="text/javascript" src="bacon.mouseover.js"></script>

  • Begin mousing over your .bacon elements to hear dat sizZzle.
